About This Converter

Speed is the rate of change of position with respect to time. It is a scalar quantity (having magnitude but no direction) measured in units of distance per time. Common speed units include: • **Metric**: meters per second (m/s), kilometers per hour (km/h) • **Imperial**: miles per hour (mph), feet per second (ft/s) • **Aviation/Maritime**: knots (nautical miles per hour) • **Aerodynamics**: Mach (multiples of the speed of sound) Speed measurements are essential in transportation, sports, physics, and engineering. Different contexts use different units - for example, wind speeds often use knots, while automotive speeds use mph or km/h.
