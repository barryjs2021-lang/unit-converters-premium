About This Converter

Baking ratios and baker's percentages are fundamental to consistent baking results. Baker's percentage expresses ingredient weights as percentages of the flour weight (flour = 100%). Understanding these ratios helps scale recipes up or down while maintaining proper proportions, and enables you to create your own recipes with confidence.
