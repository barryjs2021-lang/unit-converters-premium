Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a screening tool that measures the relationship between your height and weight to estimate body fat. While BMI is a useful indicator for most people, it doesn't directly measure body fat and may not be accurate for athletes, pregnant women, or elderly individuals.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
