Magnetic field strength is important in:
- **Electromagnetic Design**: Coil and solenoid calculations, magnetic circuit analysis
- **Materials Science**: Hysteresis loop measurement, magnetic characterization
- **Permanent Magnets**: Coercivity specification, demagnetization analysis
- **Magnetic Shielding**: Field calculation and shielding effectiveness
- **Magnetic Levitation**: Suspension system design, magnetic bearing analysis

Understanding magnetic field strength helps in:
- Designing electromagnetic devices and magnetic circuits
- Calculating magnetomotive force (MMF) and magnetic potential
- Analyzing magnetic material behavior and hysteresis
- Optimizing magnetic field distribution in devices
- Determining coercivity and magnetic material specifications
